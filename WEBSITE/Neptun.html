Neptun Facts

Neptune is the eighth planet from the Sun, making it the most distant in the solar system.
This gas giant may have formed much closer to the Sun in the early solar system history
before migrating out to its current position.Neptune is the most distant planet from the Sun.
Neptune is the smallest gas giant. A year on Neptune lasts 165 Earth years. Neptune is named
after the Roman god of the sea. Neptune has 6 faint rings.
